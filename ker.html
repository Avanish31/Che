Kerala

Per Capita Water Availability

Depth to water level Map

The depth to water level recorded in the state of Kerala during November 2012 ranged from 0.10 m bgl in Alappuzha district to 38.28 m bgl in Idukki district. It is observed 18% of the wells have less than 2 m bgl water level, mainly in coastal areas. About 32% of the wells analyzed have shown water level in the range of 2-5 m bgl.

Government Scheme
