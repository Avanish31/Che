Punjab

Per Capita Water Availability

Depth to water level Map

The depth to water level during November 2012 varies from 0.30 m bgl to 52.50 m bgl. In general the depth to water level ranges between 2-5 m bgl and 5-10 m bgl in most parts Punjab state. About 3 % of monitoring wells are showing water level in 0-2 m bgl range, about 17% monitoring wells are showing water level in 2-5 m bgl range, about 20% monitoring wells are showing water level in the range of 5-10 m bgl.

Government Scheme

Punjab Groundwater Extraction and Conservation Directions, 2023: Managed by the Punjab Water Regulation and Development Authority (PWRDA), this initiative regulates groundwater extraction with charges based on the stress level of water resources in different areas. It aims to encourage sustainable groundwater use and imposes mandatory water meter installations for large users
