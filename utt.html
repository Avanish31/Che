Uttarpradesh

Per Capita Water Availability

Depth to water level Map

The depth to water level in the state ranges between less than 0.52 m bgl in Hardoi district to 36.50 m bgl in Etawah district. Very Shallow water level ranging between 0-2 m bgl was observed in 16% of wells. Shallow water level ranging between 2-5 m bgl was observed in 42% of wells.

Government Scheme

Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM): This mission aims to provide functional household tap connections (FHTC) to rural households, ensuring sustainable water supply and quality improvement. Uttar Pradesh has made significant progress, with over 78% of rural households receiving tap connections. The mission also focuses on water conservation, solar-powered water systems, and training local women for water testing
