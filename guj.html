Per Capita Water Availability

Depth to water level Map

The depth to water level recorded in the state of Gujarat during November 2012 ranged from 0.15 m bgl in Mehsana district to 99.83 m bgl in Kutch district. It is observed that 8% of the wells analyzed have shown water level in the range of 0-2 m bgl, 27% of the wells have shown water level in the range of 2-5 m bgl.

Government Scheme

Jal Sanchay Jan Bhagidari Initiative: This scheme emphasizes community participation in constructing rainwater harvesting structures, promoting water sustainability, and aligning with the Jal Shakti Abhiyan: Catch the Rain campaign
