Per Capita Water Availability

Depth to water level Map

The depth to water level during November 2012 in the state varies from 0.20 m bgl in Dhule districts to 55.20 m bgl in Jalgaon district. Water level less than 2 m bgl are shown by about 15% wells. Depth to water level of 2 to 5 m bgl occurs in entire state with 42% of the wells falling in this range.

Government Scheme

Mukhya Mantri Water Conservation Scheme: This initiative focuses on restoring irrigation capacity and increasing water storage through the repair of water structures like minor irrigation tanks, percolation tanks, and Kolhapur-type weirs. The scheme aims to enhance water availability and irrigation efficiency across the state
