Government Scheme

Rajasthan is actively engaged in water conservation through several schemes, with the Mukhya Mantri Jal Swavlamban Abhiyan (MJSA) being the most prominent initiative. This campaign focuses on creating water harvesting structures like check dams, ponds, and percolation tanks to enhance groundwater recharge. It also emphasizes renovating traditional water bodies and promoting sustainable farming practices such as rain-fed agriculture and drip irrigation to optimize water usage
